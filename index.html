What good researchers do before they answer

Strong research doesn’t start with answers.
It starts by slowing down.

Humans clarify intent.
They judge sources.
They extract claims.
They reconcile contradictions.
Only then do they conclude.

Most AI tools skip this entire middle.
That’s why answers feel fast — and wrong.
